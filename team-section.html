About Us

The case of Covid 19, which has swept across the world including Indonesia and especially Banda Aceh, is increasing every day. In addition, before being tested positive the patient interacts with many people so that many become people in monitoring covid 19 because covid 19 symptoms will appear 2-14 days. The medical team as the vanguard in dealing with this virus is certainly overwhelmed. For this reason, our team thinks it can at least help reduce the work of the medical team by creating this website. This website will check the person being monitored every day on condition that they have to log in to this website.
