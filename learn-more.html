  • What is COVID19?

This new virus and disease were unknown before the outbreak began in Wuhan, China in

Desember 2019.The COVID-19 outbreak quickly spread around the world. It spreads the same

way other coronaviruses do, mainly through person-to-person contact. Infections range

from mild to serious.



  • How Does the New Coronavirus Spread?

Most of the time, it spreads when a sick person coughs or sneezes. They can spray droplets as

far as 6feet away. If you breathe them in or swallow them, the virus can get into your body.

Some people who have the virus don't have symptoms, but they can still spread the virus. You

You can also get the virus from touching a surface or object the virus is on, then touching

your mouth,nose, or possibly your eyes. Most viruses can live for several hours on a surface

that they land on. A study shows that the COVID-19 coronavirus can last for several hours on

various types of surfaces:

* Copper: 4 hours

* Cardboard up to 24 hours

* Plastic or stainless steel: 2 to 3 days



  • How long in the incubation person for COVID 19?

The "incubation period" means the time between catching the virus and the neginning to have

sympthoms of the diasease. Most estimates of the incubation period for COVID 19 rang for 1-14

days most commonly around five days. These estimates will be updated as more data become available



  • How Do You Prevent the Spread?

If you are in an area where it is spreading, take these steps:

1. Wash your hands often with soap and water, or clean them with an alcohol-based sanitizer

2. Practice social distancing.

3. Dont touch your face

4. Clean and disinfect.
